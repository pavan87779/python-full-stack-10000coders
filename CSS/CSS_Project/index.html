Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating in South Asia, traditionally made with rice, meat or seafood, and spices. Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia and among the South Asian diaspora, though the dish is often associated with the region's Muslim population in particular.

Fish Briyani

Fish Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish made with marinated fish pieces, basmati rice, and a blend of spices.
